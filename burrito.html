Burrito Recpies

Here we showcase some of our best recipes for burritos

Breakfest Burrito




Ingredients for avacado tomato salsa

  • 1 large avocado, peeled, pitted, and diced
  • ½ cup diced seeded tomatoes, from 1 to 2 tomatoes
  • small shallot, minced (about 2 tablespoons)
  • clove garlic, minced
  • jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced
  • tablespoon fresh lime juice, from 1 lime
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ cup fresh chopped cilantro

For The Burrito

  • 4 large eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ lb spicy sausage (such as chorizo, Italian, or anything you like), removed from casings
  • 1⅓ cups (6 oz) shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 4 (10-in) burrito-size flour tortillas
  • Vegetable oil

Fish Burrito with A guacamole cilantro sauce


Fish Buritto

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • Toppings: diced red onion, chopped cilantro, crumbled queso fresco

Guac cilantro sauce

  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steak Burrito with a Sweet and Tangy sauce


Steak Burrito

  • 1 lb skirt steak
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced avocado
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

Sweet and Tangy Sauce

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Tofu Burrito with sweet chili sauce


Tofu Burrito

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

Sweet and Chili Sauce

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tbsp sweet chili paste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp salt
